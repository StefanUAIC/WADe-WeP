About This Platform

This platform enables comprehensive management of online newspaper articles with rich metadata, provenance tracking, and semantic querying capabilities.

Key Features:

  • Full CRUD operations for newspaper articles
  • RDF/XML, JSON-LD, and RDFa export formats
  • SPARQL endpoint with predefined queries
  • Integration with external knowledge bases (DBpedia, Wikidata)
  • Multi-language support (EN, RO, ES, FR, DE)
  • QR code generation for each article

Technologies Used:

  • Node.js & Express.js (Backend)
  • Apache Jena Fuseki (Triple Store & SPARQL Endpoint)
  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Frontend)
  • Schema.org, Dublin Core, IPTC Standards (Metadata)
